# Guide for the student assistant

## How to use the folder
1. Start from [explanation.md](explanation.md) for the lecture.
2. Use [review-sheet.md](review-sheet.md) as a quick handout during the explanation.
3. Close each block with a review question.

## Recommended sequence
1. TCP/IP model and layers.
2. IP, subnets, and local routing.
3. Routing between networks and ISPs.
4. Reliable transport.
5. HTTP, REST, NAT, and TLS.

## Useful questions
- Why can't a switch replace a router?
- When do you need a subnet?
- Why is BGP important for the Internet and RIP is not?
- What is the difference between HTTP and TLS?
